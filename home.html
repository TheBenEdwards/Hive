About

Hive is part of the Universe account system, which is where one universal account can give you access to so much more than just one use.

Hive itself is a picture editing tool, that allows the multiple pictures to be editied at once on the same photo. The idea came from when converting old pictues into digital copies, as these would have to be scanned through a scanner. It would become annoying to simply scan single phtotos, so multiple photos were scanned at once. When editing these pictures on a computer, this proved to take much time, so this tool was created to allow this process to be made easy.

Enjoy!
